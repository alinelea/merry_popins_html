A Sitter When You Need One

We are an elite babysitting service in New Orleans offering child care, even when you need a sitter right away! Whether you need a babysitter last minute, temporary or part-time, our incredible network of babysitters has you and your family covered!

Our Services

Anyone with children is able to use our service. However, if you were not referred by a family who already uses the agency, we require a meet and greet at a public place, with your child/children with the first time sitter. Our number one priority is safety for not only your children, but for our amazing sitters! Sitters have the option to take or not take a job based on this meeting. Once you meet, and the sitter accepts to work for you, you are able to use our service which includes last minute babysitting at any time.

Our Sitters

19+ years old

  • Recurring Families

    Call or e-mail the agency at any time to request a sitter. Include your name, phone number, date and time you need a sitter, and number of children that will be at home. You will be contacted shortly. If you need a sitter in less than 24 hours please call or in the subject line of your e-mail type “URGENT”.

    Please note, we ask at-least a 2 hour turn around for short notice babysitting.

Our number one priority is safety for not only your children, but for our amazing sitters! Sitters have the option to take or not take a job based on this meeting.     When e-mailing, please include your name, number of children, date, time, what area you live in, and which family referred our agency to you. If you were not referred, please let us know where you heard about us and we will contact you shortly. We will set up a meet and greet for you and the sitter so you can start using our service whenever you are in need of a sitter!

    **Please note that if you would like to use the same sitter, you can request him/her when you contact us. The sitters are not to be contacted directly. This is to ensure smooth operations daily.

FAQ

  • Are your sitters background checked?

    Yes. Sitters are background and referenced check. All sitters go through a thorough an application process.

  • Are your sitters certified?

    Yes. Sitters are First Aid and CPR certified.

  • What ages can you accommodate?

    All ages. Even newborns!

  • What is the age range of your sitters?

    19+ years. All sitters are either in college or have completed a college degree.

  • Can we book last minute?

    Yes, but it depends on availability.

  • Can we share a sitter with a friend/family member?

    Of course! We have shared booking rates for this..

  • How many children can one sitter handle?

    It depends on ages but around 5 and under.

  • What about children with special needs?

    We do have sitters for children with special needs. Please email or call so we can talk about the specifics needs of your little one.

  • Can we request a specific sitter?

    Of course! It just depends on the sitter's availability if they can accommodate that time.

  • Do we pay the sitter directly?

    No. After the service, Lauren will charge your credit card for the time serviced.

  • May I tip the sitter?

    If you feel the sitter has gone above and beyond, then please do. It’s completely up to you!

  • Do you have a location? May I drop off my child(ren)?

    At this time, we only come to you. Sitters love taking children on field trips to the playground, zoo, etc. if you need them out of the house.

  • Can the sitter do school pickups?

    Yes! Some sitters already have car seats.
